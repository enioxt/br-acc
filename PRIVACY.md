## What telemetry and logs are collected

WTG may collect operational telemetry for reliability and abuse prevention:

- Request metadata (timestamp, route, status, latency).
- Rate-limiting events and security signals.
- Platform errors and diagnostic traces.

## What is not collected

In public-safe mode, WTG does not intentionally expose person-level entity outputs or personal identifier fields.

WTG does not use platform output as a criminal determination system.

## Retention windows and access controls

- Logs are retained for a limited operational window according to security and incident needs.
- Access to operational logs is restricted to authorized maintainers.
- Sensitive operational infrastructure details are kept in private repositories only.

## Abuse investigation logging statement

When abuse is suspected, WTG may preserve relevant logs for incident analysis and enforcement.  
Abuse handling follows [ABUSE_RESPONSE.md](ABUSE_RESPONSE.md) and [TERMS.md](TERMS.md).

For rights and correction handling, see [LGPD.md](LGPD.md).
